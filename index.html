Tell Me A Story

Journalism Project by Sanjana Melkote

Our lives are essentially a collection of experiences

When asked to tell a story, each person will highlight a unique moment from their life. Each story seems unrelated, but it is the very collection of random experiences that shape who we are.

Sanjana · Married at First Sight

The story of an arranged marriage is still a love story nonetheless. A married couple describes the day they met and the important decisions that followed that moment. Featuring Sneha and Keerti Melkote.

Sanjana Melkote · Kicked Out!

Two children are kicked out of a hotel room because of a fire drill! A group of friends are kicked out of a dorm room because someone gets sick! Where are they going to go? Listen and join the adventure! Featuring Sanjana Melkote and Srikanth Nampoothiri
